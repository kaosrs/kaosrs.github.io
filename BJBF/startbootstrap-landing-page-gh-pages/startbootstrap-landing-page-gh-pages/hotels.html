Valle Escondido Resort Hotel & Spa:

A beautiful upscale hotel located very close to the festival venue in Boquete's most luxurious residential community. Just a two minute walk to the music.

Other Recommended Hotels in Boquete

(discount details are still being worked out)



Hotel Oasis:

Central, quiet location on banks of the Caldera river, great restaurant. Just a 3 minutes walk to the centre of town.


Villa Marita:

Villa Marita is a privately owned Country Hotel located in the mountains of Boquete in the province of Chiriquí, Panama, just 10 minutes from town center, 45 minutes from Enrique Malek International Airport. The Villa Marita is a great getaway, nice relaxing vacation in the Panamanian country side.

Hotel Isla Verde:

Centrally located, very quiet, Cabaña-style rooms, beautiful garden, 4 minutes walk to town centre, And within walking distance to the festival venue.


Stone Cabins:

Located on the south side of town but within an easy walk to the town center the Stone Cabins each offer two comfortable rooms plus a kitchen. Great for families or small groups.


Panamonte Inn & Spa:

The first hotel in Boquete. 5 minutes walk from city center. Charming, colonial style. Beautiful fireplace bar and garden. Very quiet, great restaurant.
