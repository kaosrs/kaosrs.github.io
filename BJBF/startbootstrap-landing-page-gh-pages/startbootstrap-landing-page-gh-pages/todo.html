WHAT TO SEE & DO

What to See & Do in Boquete


Whether you like outdoor adventures, rest & relaxation, or good food, Boquete offers something for everyone!

Check out the links below to discover what awaits you in Boquete:

  • Visit Panama | The official website of the Panamanian tourist authority with all you want and need to know about traveling to and within Panama

  • www.Boquete.ning.com | All you want to know about what's actually going on in Boquete

  • Boquete Travel Guide | Another great source of information about anything Boquete and Chiriqui

  • Boquete Weather | All you need to know about our weather

  • Finca Dos Jefes | A great tour of the Finca dos Jefes. Our official partner for Boquete coffee-tours!

  • Boquete Outdoor Adventures | All kinds of outdoor adventures while you're here...

  • Habla Ya | Ecotourism tours in the Boquete area...and much more!

  • Boquete Tree Trek | Zip-lining adventures

  • Explora Ya | A site for tour packages and adventure tours

  • Lonely Planet | Lonely Planet reviews of tours & things to do

  • Boquete Tree Trek | A wealth of information about what to do and to see in Chiriqui

  • The Haven | Give yourself a treat after all that jazz & blues....

  • Boquete Restaurants | Boquete boasts an astonishing number of good restaurants. Here's a listing with detailed reviews

  • Panama Info | More information about everything Panama

  • Chiriqui Chatter | A very useful blog about what's going on in David and beyond

  • Chiriqui.org | Touristic info about the province of Chiriqui

  • TripAdvisor | Comprehensive information about Panama weather, flights, car rentals,hotels,things to do etc

  • The Panama News | An English language news blog about Panamanian politics, culture, events, real estate etc.
