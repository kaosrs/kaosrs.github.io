The Tradition Continues:

Experience everything Boquete has to offer as it comes alive with the sounds of jazz and blues at the 2018 Boquete Jazz & Blues Festival. Join the beautiful town of Boquete February 23-25 and experience two stages of live jazz & blues, local food, drinks, shopping, and entertainment.

Since first taking place in 2007 the Boquete Jazz & Blues Festival has been the biggest music event in the province of Chiriqui. Since 2012, the event was also the first ever festival in Panama to showcase first-class international Blues acts in addition to notable Panamanian and Latin American artists featuring Jazz and Latin American music styles, and they were a great success!
