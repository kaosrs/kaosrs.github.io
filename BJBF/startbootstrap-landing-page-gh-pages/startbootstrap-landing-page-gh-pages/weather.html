WEATHER

Boquete Weather:





February is in the middle of the dry season which lasts from late December until late April. Still, occasional light rain (the famous Boquete "Bajareque", a very fine mist which often produces wonderful rainbows) cannot be excluded. Temperatures in the Boquete area usually stay in the 70's all year round with highs in the lower 80's. Late evenings can get a bit chilly sometimes. It gets much hotter in the lowlands near the oceans, of course. After all, we're in the tropics here, right?
