MUSIC FOR SCHOOLS

BJBF Music for Schools program:

The Boquete Jazz & Blues Festival Foundation donates 100% of profits of the festival to local schools in order to support and improve musical education, and to find and develop local musical talents. The foundation has donated $7,300 to local schools since 2012!

BJBF continues to support musical eduction in local schools. Scroll down to view our donations throughout the years, and check out our Music for Schools Gallery page for more!

2016:

On November, 9 2016 Mr. John Wolf, director of The Boquete Jazz & Blues Festival, presented a check in the amount of $1,000.00 to the Colegio Bilingue el Buen Pastor in Boquete.

2015:

The students of Academia Internacional de Boquete performed a musical "Grease" with financial support from the foundation of the Boquete Jazz & Blues Festival.

2014:

On June 18, 2014 Mr. Hans Lebuser, director of BJBF 2015 and treasurer of the Boquete Jazz & Blues Foundation handed a $2000 check to the principals of the Academia Internacional de Boquete (AIB), Mrs. Carla Rigau and Mrs. Melissa Vallarino.

This donation was specifically to be used for the realization of three projects:

  1. A 3-day musical show of students of the AIB at the Boquete communal theater BCP in December 2014
  2. A musical-theatrical show at the opening of the planned art show "Art of Music' which ran at the Boquete library parallel to the 2015 festival
  3. A short musical-theatrical performance in one of the intermissions of the 2015 Boquete Jazz & Blues Festival

2013:

$4500 was donated to the Academia Internacional and the Escuela Buen Pastor in Boquete.
