THE VENUE

The Festival Venue



Thanks to our followers, the Boquete Jazz & Blues Festival is a very successful event. Over the years, the festival has grown and attendance has increased. Valle Escondido has been the festival's home for the past few years, however, a larger venue was needed to accomodate more festival goers and provide even more music.



Boquete's "Festival de las Flores" fairgrounds will be our new home for 2018. Here we will be able to take the festival to the next level by including art exhibits, vendor stands and of course a variety of food and beverage. The fairgrounds offer us greater seating capacity and room to delight our public, including two stages with continuous music.
